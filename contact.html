Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?
Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also check your order status in your account dashboard.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy for unused items in their original packaging. Contact our customer service team to initiate a return.
Do you ship internationally?
Currently, we ship within Nepal. We're working on expanding our shipping to international destinations soon.
How can I become a wholesale customer?
For wholesale inquiries, please contact us at wholesale@glamify.com or call our business development team.
